Let's go on a adventure!

Where to being! My all time favourite book is "His dark material"-triology. I remembere reading the first book in just one day, I never let it go! The second book, I "took it a bit easy" and finished in one week. The last book, i had to live more of a normal life, my parents got worried! So, took about 1 month. I loved every minute. I was so well written, so intresting, and so many layers that you might not notice at first. I would love to have my own Daemon, an animal that is basically your soul, that follows you around and can't be to far away from without it hurting you. Such a romantic view of your soul.

I can't talk about favourite Fantasybooks without mentioning Harry Potter. What a book-serie! The first two books my father read out loud to me and my brother. It was so cozy, and we were so involved in the story, it got harder to get ut to go to sleep. The third book, we were old enough to wanting to read ourselves. I love the magical world she describes to us. It's so detailt but at the same time always brings the story forward. It's smart that they are in the school during most of the books, which makes it easier to "explain the magical world" to us readers, whitout us thining about it. I remember reading until book number five, Harry Potter and the Order of Phoenix, then i got so upset I decided that nope, this was it, I can't handle whats happening! I think it took me five years until I actually read the last three books haha! But loved them anyway. I also think that she, the author, have been supersmart to write the books so you can "grow" with the books. The language and story is much easier to read in the first books, then gets more and more advanced and "scarier". Fantastic writing!

The last bok I want to mention is not a bookserie, but the book "Ready player one". If any fellow nerd haven't read that book, I urge you to do it! Not only is it a complete nostalgic 80's video game and popculture fiesta, it's also tells a very interesting story about a future dystopia. A dystopia that doesnt seem so far away from reality to my opinion! I loved to talk and and discuss that book, especially with this rapid technology we have today and with the growing online communities that already exist.
